### Form classes
Fixed form sizes: 
`form--s` 400px,
`form--m` 800px,
`form--l` 1200px

To wrap it around a card:
`form--card`

Inline:
`form--inline`

To limit a form submit trigger, add: `form--submit-limit`
and
```form.js
import Fast from 'project-fast';
Fast.Form();
```
